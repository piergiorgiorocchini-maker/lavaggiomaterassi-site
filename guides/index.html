How to use these guides

Read the guide, check the surface, then send photos if you need a real assessment

1. Identify the surface

Mattress, carpet, moquette, upholstery, sofa, cushion or mixed textile surfaces all require different expectations.

2. Check stains and odours

Note what happened, how long the issue has been present and whether any product has already been used.

3. Take useful photos

Send wide shots, close-ups, surface details and the area where the work would be carried out.

4. Ask before improvising

A quick assessment can prevent avoidable damage from aggressive detergents, over-wetting or unsuitable cleaning attempts.
