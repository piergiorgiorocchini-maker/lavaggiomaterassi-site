FAQ

Before you call

Do you clean only mattresses, or also upholstery and carpets?

The yacht service focuses on mattresses, cabin textiles, upholstery, carpets and moquette. The best starting point is to send photos and describe the location, surface and timing.

Can I send photos before booking?

Yes. Photos are strongly recommended. Send wide shots, close-ups, surface type, stains, odours, yacht location and deadline before charter or owner arrival.

Do you promise complete stain removal?

No. Textile results depend on material, age of the stain, previous treatments, humidity and general condition. The assessment is designed to be practical and realistic.

Which yacht areas do you cover?

The main focus is Sardinia, with particular attention to Costa Smeralda, Porto Cervo, Porto Rotondo, Portisco, Olbia and other premium coastal areas depending on availability.
